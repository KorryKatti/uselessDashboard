## Strategic Value Pillars

### 1. Predictive Sales Quantum Engine (PSQE)
Utilizing a `GradientBoostingRegressor` (which sounds significantly more expensive than it is), we forecast `units_sold` with a precision that borders on the clairvoyant. We factor in seasonality, promotion flags, and "vibe checks" (lag features) to ensure your stock levels never exceed your ambition.

### 2. Categorical Hyper-Segmentation (CHS)
Is your product "Hot," "Steady," or "Low-Performing"? Our `RandomForestClassifier` doesn't just label data; it passes judgment. By analyzing sales velocity and profit margins, we provide a deterministic framework for your retail existentialism.

### 3. Unsupervised Human Clustering (UHC)
We don't see "customers"; we see "clusters." Using `KMeans` dimensionality reduction (PCA-enhanced), we group biological entities into 3-5 distinct segments. This allows for hyper-targeted marketing, or as we call it, "Algorithmic Destiny."

### 4. Recursive Knowledge Synthesis (The "Self-Learning" Loop)
Our proprietary "Self-Learning" technology (a Python script that calls `.fit()` whenever new CSVs are detected) ensures that as new data arrives, the AI evolves. It is a digital Ouroboros, consuming data to produce "Intelligence™."

## How to Use

### First Run (with sample data)
1. Train the model: `python pipeline_sales.py`
2. Launch: `streamlit run dashboard.py`
3. You'll see pre-loaded sample data across all panels

### Upload Your Own Data
1. Click the **Upload Sales CSV** and **Upload Products CSV** buttons in the sidebar
2. Your data flows through all charts and tables automatically
3. Click **Retrain Model** in the sidebar to retrain on your data

### Reading the Dashboard
- **Sales Forecast**: Actual vs predicted monthly sales. Green line = model, blue line = reality
- **Product Performance**: Top/bottom products ranked by total units sold
- **Customer Clusters**: PCA visualization of customer segments by spend and frequency
- **Feature Importance**: What factors matter most (price, weekday, promotions, etc.)

### CSV Format
Expand the "CSV Format Help" section in the sidebar for exact column requirements.
